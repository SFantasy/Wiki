UITabBarItem

自定义UITabBarItem的背景图

UITabBarItem *itemOne = [tabBar.items objectAtIndex:0];
UIImage *itemOneImage = [UIImage imageNamed:@"Shape.png"];
itemOneImage = [itemOneImage imageWithRenderingMode:UIImageRenderingModeAlwaysOriginal];
itemOne.selectedImage = itemOneImage;
[itemOne setImage:itemOneImage];

About main.m

In 99.99% of the iOS apps you’ll encounter as a developer, you never need to edit main.m; therefore it is stored away in the Supporting Files folder.
